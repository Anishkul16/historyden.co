The Kauravas were 100, everyone knows. However, these 100 brothers had a sister too. Her name was Duhsala. She was the daughter of King Dhritrashtra and Queen Gandhari (character) and was the only sister of the Pandavas as well. So they were 100 brothers and 1 sister, together 101 children.
